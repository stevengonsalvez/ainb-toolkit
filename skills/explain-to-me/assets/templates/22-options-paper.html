Options Paper · trade-off analysis

Real-time delivery for live updates

Date2026-05-02 Author@stevie For decision byPlatform Eng · 2026-05-16 Draft · for review
01

Problem

The current "poll every 2s" path costs us ~38% of API CPU and produces a noticeably stale UI when more than ~200 sessions are active. We need a delivery mechanism that scales to 5k concurrent sessions with sub-second perceived latency, without doubling our paging surface.

This paper compares four options against the same scenario: 5k concurrent sessions, 20 events/sec/session at peak, 99% same-region clients, 1% mobile/flaky.
